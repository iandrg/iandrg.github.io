Graph-enhanced reasoning

Knowledge graphs model entities (companies, tickers, executives), relationships (supplier, owner, counterparty), and attributes (KPIs, filings, risk flags). Path-based retrieval allows queries like:

Query example:
"Find direct suppliers of Company X with rising leverage in last 12 months and summarize related risk statements in most recent 10-K."

Path-based reasoning:
Company X -> Supplier (edge) -> Supplier's leverage (attribute/time-series) -> 10-K risk passage (source)
